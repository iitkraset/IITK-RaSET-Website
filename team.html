About IITK RaSET

IITK RaSET was initially established in 2022 as a Science and Technology Council project by Atharva Dehdariya, along with his junior team members Nandan and Shraman. Over time, Nandan and Shraman assumed leadership of the council project. In 2024, the initiative was officially transformed into a recognized Science and Technology Council team at IIT Kanpur. The first leadership team of RaSET comprised Prabhu Safi, Aniket Nandi, Rangan Pal, and Riya Gupta.
